Textile Instructions

Move the shapes around until you find their correct places by dragging them.

Pan around the textile by dragging the solid background.

Tap/click to pause.

Double tap/click to change zoom. Generally, zoom in to better read the text, and zoom out to see the picture of how the shapes fit together.

The revealed text behind the shapes will be a clue as to where the shapes should go.

No one said it would be easy... so good luck!
